Utility classes providing basic services to applications. They are designed to help with error processing and with the handling of operating parameters and simple files.

In most cases, methods avoid throwing checked exceptions but explicitly document exceptions. Illegal null pointers result in an IllegalArgumentException and are documented implicitly as part of the comment on the relevant parameter.

Exceptions thrown by utility classes come with a detailed message and a cause chain making it possible in most situations to easily understand what went wrong.
